# Weather-aware shopping guidance

Rainy season shopping tips:
- Use waterproof laptop sleeves and backpack rain covers for commuting.
- Prefer IP-rated earbuds and smartwatches for outdoor use.
- Keep silica gel packs in camera and electronics storage bags.

Hot weather shopping tips:
- Choose cooling pads for gaming laptops.
- Use surge protectors and proper ventilation for desktop setups.
- Keep batteries and power banks away from direct sunlight.

Monsoon power reliability:
- UPS units protect desktops and routers during short outages.
- Offline backups are recommended before firmware updates during unstable weather.

Support policy quick facts:
- Standard shipping: 3 to 5 business days.
- Return window: 30 days from delivery date.
